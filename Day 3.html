Top 10 loved Scratch projects


10. The Ninja, by @Will_Wam: 40,398 ❤

The Ninja is a third-person quest platfomer created by "@Will_Wam". Based on ninjas, you have to avoid the danger and complete all the tasks to finish the game. It also has many continuations such as Ninja 2, Ninja 3 all the way to Ninja 3D.



9. Hill Climb Racing v1.0, by @griffpatch: 40,959 ❤

This is a Scratch remake of the popular game Hill Climb Racing made by the popular Scratch user "@griffpatch" the game producer company called Fingersoft. The excution of this project is very good as it includes a feauture that saves your score to a leaderboard that includes 179 of the highest scores in the game. There are 2 levels each with a seperate scoreboard making this project very sophisticated.



8. Epic Ninja v1.12,by @griffpatch: 41,677 ❤

This is yet another game based on ninjas and by "@griffpatch" but it's a bit different from the previous 2 projects. It includes AI enemy and includes weapons and collectibles. It is a very attractive project with beautiful graphics and appealing design.



7. Cloud Platformer v1.35, by @griffpatch: 48,260 ❤

This is a platformer that is like no other... yet another project by "@griffpatch". This is multiplayer platfromer alllowing you to interact with other Scratch users, it also involves checkpoints evolving or transforming your player into many different imaginary things, such as Pikachu, the Nyan cat and a T-Rex.
